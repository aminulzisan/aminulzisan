Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I hire a security-focused developer?

Security isn't just a feature; it's a foundation. Fixing a security breach after launch costs 10x more than preventing it. I build applications that are secure by design, protecting your user data and your reputation from day one.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Absolutely. I offer various support packages to ensure your application remains updated, secure, and performant. Whether it's bug fixes, security patches, or feature updates, I've got you covered.

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary based on complexity. A standard business website might take 1-2 weeks, while a full-stack web application with security auditing could take 4-8 weeks. We'll establish a clear timeline during our initial discovery call.

Do you work with startups?

Yes, I love working with startups! I understand the need for speed and scalability. I can help you build an MVP that is robust enough to handle growth but flexible enough to evolve.
